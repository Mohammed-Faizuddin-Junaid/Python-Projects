# Student Performance Analysis
This project analyses student exam scores in different subjects using Python,Pandas and Matplotlib.pyplot
# Key Findings and Visualizations
## 1. Subject Performance Overview(Bar Graph)
Our analysis of the average scores across all students revealed the following insights:
* **Highest Performing Subject:** **Physics**,showing the strongest overall student understanding.  
* **Lowest Performing Subject:** **Math**,indicating an area where students needs additional support.
![Subject Averages](Bar_graph.png)
## 2. Math vs. Physics Relationship (Scatter Plot)
We need a scatter plot to analyze the relationship between student performance in Math and Physics.
* **Observation** The data points show an upward trend,indicating a **positive correlation** between Math and Physics scores. Students who score higher in Nath genraly tend to perform better in Physics as well.
![Math vs Physics Scatter Plot](./Scatter_plot.png)
### 3. Student Performance Overview (Heatmap)
The heatmap provides a complete visual grid of every student's performance across all subjects, using color intensity  to represent score ranges (darker blue represent higher scores).
* **Observation:** By scanning the rows,we can instantly identify top performing students (rows dominated by dark blue) versus students who need comprehensive academic support across multiplle subjects (rows dominated by light blue or white).![Performance Heatmap](./Heat_Map.png)
## Conclusion  & Next Steps
1.**Targeted Interventions:** Since  **Math** has the lowest overall average,the academic team should introduce tutoring for this subject.

2.**Leveraging Strerngths:** Students performing exceptionally well in Physics can be pared as peer-mentors for students struggling with  foundational Math concepts.
